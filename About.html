About Dr.Subhash Singh

Dr.Subhash Singh is a faculty member of the Department of Mechanical and Automation Engineering,IGDTUW since 2022. He Pursued his B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering(2003) from Kamla Nehru Institute of Technology,Sultanpur,Uttar Pradesh.He Obtained his M.Tech Degree (2007) from NIT Kurukshetra and then he Obtained his Phd Degree (2017) from IIT Roorkee.Prior joining IGDTUW,he started his journey at Mody Institute of Technology & Science (Deemed University) as an Assistant Professor from 2007 to 2011 and then Worked in Graphic Era University (Deemed University) as an Assistant Professor from 2011 to 2012,Worked in Vyas Institute of Higher Education, Jodhpur, Rajasthan, India as an Assistant Professor from 2016 to 2017,worked as an Assistant Professor in Mechanical Engineering Department in Lovely Professional University, Jalandhar, Punjab, India from 2017 to 2018,worked as an Assistant Professor in Manufacturing Engineering Department in National Institute of Technology Jamshedpur, Jamshedpur, Jharkhand, India since 2018 to 2022.

His fields of interests are Modification of Nano materials,thin coating,Synthesis of Nanocrystalline spinel,MMCs,Synthesis of 2D Materials,Friction Stir Processing,Un-conventional Machining of Biodegradable Materials.Currently,He is working in fabrication of lightweight materials like Mg and its alloys for various applications on micro and macro scale.
